Background

EasyPharm is a Drug prescription delivery service that allows patients to either request for a new prescription or renew their prescription. EasyPharm is a reliable, convenient feature that helps patients have access to wide range of drugs, Information about drugs including their side effects and compositions as well as precautions to be taken before, during and after use, EasyPharm offers the possibility of saving money, time and energy. It also help improve medication adherence.
