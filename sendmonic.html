About Client

Send Monie is a mobile application designed to provide users with the easiest, fastest and most effective ways to pay, send, receive and borrow money through peer to peer transfers.

Background

Users find it difficult and exhausting to make daily transactions due to the complexity that comes with most FinTech Applications. The goal is to design a mobile application that is easy, fast and efficiently allows users to send, receive, pay and borrow money through peer to peer transfers.
