About Client

EAF is a platform where you can donate or support the less privilege with money or resources to empower children of Local communities.

Background

Ewaade foundation is a non-governmental organization who’s aim is to support and empower the less privileged through access to quality education, health care and diet. The targeted beneficiaries are the underprivileged children who do not have access to quality education, health care and good diet. EAF haS the responsibility to ensure that our goals and objectives are achieved affectionately and with utmost importance.

Research

Individuals and groups need trusted fundraising platforms to help pool resources together for different reasons. People like donating to a good cause but they want to be sure that their money is actually used for the purpose they are donating it for.
