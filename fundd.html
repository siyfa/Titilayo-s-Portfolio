About Client

Fundd is a fundraising website that offers seamless pooling of funds from a wide range of audience and donors.

Background

People go through a lot of circumstances which can be planned or unplanned, foreseen or unforeseen. In difficult unforeseen times, we rely on others for help to navigate through life after all a popular saying is that 'we need each other to survive'. We need the warmth, the love and presence of people around us to make the journey through life easier and it's better to go through life together than to do it alone.

Research

Individuals and groups need trusted fundraising platforms to help pool resources together for different reasons. People like donating to a good cause but they want to be sure that their money is actually used for the purpose they are donating it for.
